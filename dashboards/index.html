Case Study: data.gov.uk
Open Spending Report

Open Knowledge Services designed and built the Departmental Spend Report Dashboard for Data.Gov.UK.

The Dashboard tracks performance by Departments across government in publishing key spending information on time and in line with some of the UK Government's flagship transparency commitments.

The Dashboard is public in-line with the overall transparency agenda, and enables both citizens and key governmental decision makers to understand and drive performance in this area.
